Ymir father of the frost giants

Ymir is what's known in smite as a support. He assists another player that is less powerful early in the game, but can become more powerful later. Many players also use this god to deal immense amounts of damage if the person they would be supporting can handle the lane by their self. In Norse Mythology Ymir was a primeval being made of elemental venom. The gods Odin, Vili, and Ve, killed him and used his body to make the Earth. His blood became the sea, from his bones the hills, from his skull the heavens, from his hair the trees, from his brains the clouds, and from his eyebrows the middle realm known as Midgard, which is where the humans live. In Smite, Ymir is a powerful guardian, with incredibly high potential damage.

Abilities

  • Frostbite:

    (Passive ability) When Ymir damages an enemy with an ability, they are given frostbite, and will take double damage from Ymir's basic attacks for a short time

  • Ice Wall:

    (First ability) Ymir raises a wall of ice from the ground that blocks both friendly, and enemy movement.

  • Glacial Strike:

    (Second ability) Ymir smashes his club on the ground, causing a spikes of ice to explode from the gound, which do damage, and slow enemies. The ability has a slight delay between when it is cast, and when it does damage, so be sure to aim it well.

  • Frost Breath:

    (Third ability) Ymir breathes out a blast of icy wind which freezes enemies, stunning them. This is Ymir's most important ability, it not only alows him to use his glacial strike wih impunity, it also allows teammates to hit frozen enemies with abilities that would ordinarily be hard to hit.

  • Shard of Ice:

    (Ultimate ability) Ymir is frozen in place, and charges up a massive explosion over 3 seconds. Enemies in the radius are slowed until it detonates, and when it does, it does a considerable amount of damage. It can be detonated early by canceling it, but it will do less damage.

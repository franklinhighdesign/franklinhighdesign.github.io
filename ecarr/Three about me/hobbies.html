Hobbies

I enjoy playing video games. Overwatch, Stardew Valley, Smite and Team fortress 2 are my favorite games of all time. I usually spend 6-8 hours per day on videogames.

Reaper is my favorite character in Overwatch. He is a high damage, short rage attacker. He has a teleport and invinciblity. His Ultimate does very high damage in a semi-large circle.

The Soldier in TF2 is my favorite class to play. He has high HP, a rocket launcher, a shotgun and a melee. He can use the rocket launcher to gain insane mobility and height. Also his rocketlauncher does very high damage.

Loki is my favorite god to play in Smite. He can go invisible, spawn a decoy of himself and increase his damage for the next attack. Loki is a very high DPS god but very weak and has low health. His ultimate is where he teleports on someones back and stuns them giving him access to a free high damage attack.

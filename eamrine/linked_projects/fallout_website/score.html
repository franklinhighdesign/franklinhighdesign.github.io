Fallout 4 receives a 9/10 from me. I really want to give a 10/10 because of my love for Fallout, but it's just not quite perfect. The game that you're intended to experience truly is quite fun. Combat is well-done and being able to build your own settlement is pretty entertaining. The gameplay keeps you wanting to play. The bugs can drive you away however. In the end, it's a great game that I would recommend to all.
